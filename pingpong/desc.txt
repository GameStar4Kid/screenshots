
Ping Pong Multiplayer


Ping Pong Multiplayer is the classic game of pong reinvented.

Compete with your friends and family or work together and reach new high scores!
To play, just hold your finger on your racket and drag it up and down, keep hitting the ball for as long as possible!

Collect coins and unlock cool balls or fun logo's for your side of the screen.

FEATURES:
* There are 2 modes: play with AI or friend.
* Solid and fun physics!
* Simple yet enjoyable game play!
* Work together and beat your high score!
* Improve your sense of timing and reflexes!
* Collect Coins to unlock new things!
* Addictive and fast paced
